Hi! My name is Chirayu Salgarkar (he/him), and I am a graduate student at Rochester Institute of Technology, where I am affiliated with the Safe AI Lab. I earned my undergraduate degree at Mercer University, where I studied biomedical engineering and mathematics.

Broadly, I study how techniques in mathematics can control highly nonlinear systems, such as those in current AI technologies. Currently, I am interested in _multi-agent reinforcement learning_ and _optimal transport_. Previously, I spent time doing software development at the Georgia Tech Research Institute, as a prosthetic fitter in Cambodia, and as a research assistant at Georgia Tech's LIDAR-FACTS Lab, where I worked on bipedal robot communication. I am an alumnus of the Budapest Semesters in Mathematics. As of Summer 2026, I am a Graduate ML developer intern at a startup in Cambridge, Massachusetts, working on multi-agent reinforcement learning for digital twins.
